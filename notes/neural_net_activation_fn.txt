Neural network consists of neurons. Neurons are simply which consists digit.
Each Neuron takes input, applies weight and adds bias and produces output/a value.
The raw value is often called activation (before applying function) or pre-activation.

But "activation" in many context means the final output of the neuron after applying activation function.
So, ACTIVATION = the output value of a neuron.

An activation function is a function applied to the neuron's output.

the apply activation function: a=f(z)
